### Manage Telemetry Data Quotas in Your Sawmills Account Using the Following Tutorial

#### Overview

With this feature, you can:

* Ensure your system stays efficient by avoiding overconsumption of resources
* Keep data usage within predefined limits to prevent performance issues
* Stay informed with alerts when you approach or exceed your quota
* Optimize resource use and manage costs effectively

This system gives you the tools to balance comprehensive data insights with the operational needs of your organization.
